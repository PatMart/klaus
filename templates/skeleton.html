GikiLog: A Geeky Blog/Wiki Hybrid

Welcome to my personal corner of the web. This site is a blog/wiki hybrid.
  • If you're a blogger, the tree on the left is a tag hierarchy, and each tag has a summarizing page attached to it (which I update with each blogpost for that tag)
  • If you're a wiki fan, the tree on the left is the wiki page hierarchy, and the story on the right is the 'recent changes' (and I write big ones)
  • If you're a gitter, commit messages are blog posts, and the files they update are the wiki pages
-- Arnout Engelen
